Fallout 4

"Fallout 4" is an action role-playing game developed by Bethesda Game Studios, released in 2015. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, the game is set in the Boston area known as the Commonwealth. Players assume the role of a survivor of Vault 111, who emerges after 200 years of cryogenics to find a world devastated by nuclear war. With an engaging narrative, the game offers freedom of exploration, intense combat, and the ability to shape the fate of the world through the player's choices.


Minecraft

"Minecraft is a sandbox game created by Mojang Studios, released in 2011. Set in an infinite pixelated world, players can explore, build and survive in a procedurally generated environment. With open and flexible mechanics, the game offers creative freedom for players to build impressive structures, explore caves and face diverse challenges. With game modes such as Survival, Creative and Adventure, "Minecraft" captivates players of all ages, allowing them to create their own virtual world and tell their own stories.


Fortnite

"Fortnite is a battle royale game developed by Epic Games that was released in 2017. Set in a colorful, cartoonish world, the game pits up to 100 players against each other on an island where they compete to be the last one standing. With unique building mechanics, players can collect resources and build defensive structures throughout the game. In addition to the Battle Royale mode, Fortnite also features creative game modes, where players can build and share their own maps and game modes. With seasonal events, frequent updates, and a vibrant community, Fortnite has become a cultural phenomenon and one of the most popular games today.


Destiny

"Destiny 2 is the sequel to Bungie's 2017 first-person shooter. Continuing the story of the first game, players continue to defend the last city on Earth as Guardians, facing new alien threats. With a cinematic campaign, expanded cooperative and competitive activities, Destiny 2 offers an even more immersive gameplay experience. In addition, the game continues to receive regular content updates, expansions and seasonal events, keeping players engaged and exploring its vast universe.
